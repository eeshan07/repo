Question 2: Verify Cayley-Hamilton theorem for the matrix. And hence, find ùê¥

MATLAB Code:
clc
clear
A=input('Enter the Matrix:');
%Verification of Cayley-Hamilton theorem
cf=poly(A);
n=length(cf);
CHT=cf(1)*A^(n-1);
for i=2:n
CHT=CHT+cf(i)*A^(n-i);
end
disp('R.H.S of C-H Theorem:');
disp(round(CHT));
%To find the inverse
INV=cf(1)*A^(n-2);
for i=2:n-1
INV=INV+cf(i)*A^(n-i-1);
end
INV=INV/(-cf(n));
disp('Inverse of A:')
disp(INV)
